Welcome to SysReviewer - The Systematic Review App

Powered by PubMed, SysReview gives you the tools to create, organise and track your systematic review. Create and edit your search queries making sure you only have the results that are important to you.

Review and approve papers at each stage of your systematic review. SysReviewer gives you quick access to each stage of your systematic review.

The SysReviewer Systematic Review Building Blocks

  • Create/edit Multiple Reviews
  • Create multiple queries, test your results, select the best query for your review
  • Abstract Pool - Review,approve or reject your reviews papers based on their abstracts
  • Document Pool - Visit the full paper, select the papers that are relevant to your review
  • Final Pool - View the final papers that are necessary for your review
